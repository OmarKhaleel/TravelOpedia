Our story

20 Years of Experience

My husband and I have always been fond of traveling around the world and exploring all these sites we only see in Tv. Therefore, we came up with the idea of TravelOpedia, an agency that makes the world of travel welcoming to people of all backgrounds and abilities. As much as it makes us happy when we are traveling and visiting new places, we love seeing other people live and get to experience this feeling with us as well.

Our agency consists of 6 staff members all of whom have been around the world and got to experience the good and bad parts of traveling, that is what makes us unique! You get to listen from traveling experts and they can link you up with the best travel guides wherever you go.
