Mount Kalamangaro

Traveling to Africa to hike Mount Kalamangaro was the most difficult challenge yet the most exciting experience I've ever encountered. Mount Kalamangaro is the highest mountain in all of Africa and the fourth highest in the World at a height of 120 meters above sea level! Not everyone was able to reach the top as climbing the mountain had many challenges, the worst one was the extreme coldness. When I got to reach the top I knew that I was capable if doing anything if I just put my mind into it. I will never forget this wonderful experience.
